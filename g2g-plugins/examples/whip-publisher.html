glass2glass WHIP publisher

Publishes an animated canvas as a WebRTC video track to a WHIP endpoint, so g2g can read it back over WHEP. No camera or microphone: the canvas is captured directly, so the browser shows no permission prompt. With mediamtx, publishing to http://host:8889/browserpub/whip is read back at http://host:8889/browserpub/whep.
